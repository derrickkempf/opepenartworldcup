Rules

Short version — there is no long version

40 artworks, single elimination. Rounds run 40 → 20 → 10 → 5, a play-in trims 5 to 4, then semi-finals and a final.

Every match is 90 minutes played in 90 seconds — two 45-second halves (1 second = 1 minute) with a halftime break, back to back all tournament.

Vote while the match is live. One pick per match per device. The side with the most votes at full time advances; ties go to the higher seed.

Taste Points reward participation: +25 per vote, +100 when your pick wins, small bonuses on the Earn page. TP lives on your device.
